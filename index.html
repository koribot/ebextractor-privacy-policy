User Privacy Policy

eBexTractor's commitment to your privacy:

eBexTractor values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how eBexTractor collects, uses, and safeguards your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Information eBexTractor Collects:

eBexTractor only sends HTML data of eBay search results and items to our server for processing. We do not collect or store any user-specific information.

How eBexTractor Uses Your Information:

eBexTractor sends HTML data to our server for processing to extract structured data such as prices, keywords, and other relevant information. We do not use or store any user-specific data.

Data Security at eBexTractor:

eBexTractor ensures the security of data transmission between our extension and server to protect user privacy. We do not store any user information on our servers.

Data Sharing by eBexTractor:

eBexTractor does not share user-specific data with third parties. We only process HTML data to extract structured information and do not retain any user information.

Your Choices with eBexTractor:

Since eBexTractor does not collect or store any user information, there are no options or choices related to data collection or management for users of our extension.
